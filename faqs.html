Basics

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on "Sign Up" at the top of the homepage and fill in your details.

What is this website about?

We offer a wide range of products including electronics, clothing, accessories, and home goods.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices.

Account

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" at the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team with your account details.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Is it safe to use my credit card?

Yes, all transactions are encrypted with secure SSL protocols for maximum security.

Orders

How can I track my order?

You can track your order through the "Orders" section in your account dashboard.

Can I change my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify your order within 2 hours of placing it by contacting our support team.

Shipping

What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs vary depending on location and product weight. Free shipping is available for orders above $50.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide.

Returns

How do I return a product?

You can initiate a return through your account dashboard under "Returns" or contact support for assistance.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned product is received.
